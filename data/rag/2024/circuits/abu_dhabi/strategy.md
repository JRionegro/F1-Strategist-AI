# Abu Dhabi Grand Prix - Strategy Guide

## Circuit Overview

Yas Marina Circuit is a 5.281 km street-style circuit with 16 turns.
The race distance is 58 laps (306.183 km total).

### Key Characteristics

- **Sector 1**: High-speed, flowing turns (T1-T7)
- **Sector 2**: Technical section with chicanes (T8-T11)
- **Sector 3**: Hotel section with tight corners (T12-T16)

## Pit Stop Strategy

### Optimal Windows

| Strategy | Lap Range | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| 1-Stop | L20-25 | Medium-Hard works best |
| 2-Stop | L15-18 + L35-40 | Soft-Medium-Medium viable |

### Pit Lane Time Loss

Pit lane time loss is approximately **20.5 seconds**.
This makes 2-stop strategies less favorable unless:

- High degradation occurs
- Safety car opportunity arises
- Track position battle requires fresh tires

## Tire Degradation

Abu Dhabi typically shows moderate degradation:

- **Front tires**: 0.08-0.12 sec/lap
- **Rear tires**: 0.05-0.08 sec/lap

The smooth surface and evening conditions help preserve tires.

## Overtaking Opportunities

### DRS Zones

1. **DRS Zone 1**: Main straight (after T16)
2. **DRS Zone 2**: After T5 (short zone)

### Key Overtaking Points

- **Turn 1**: Late braking zone, inside line preferred
- **Turn 6**: Possible with DRS assist
- **Turn 8**: Tight hairpin, divebombs possible

## Safety Car Probability

Historical SC probability: **35-40%**

Common SC triggers:
- T14 barrier contact
- First lap incidents at T1/T2
- Technical failures in final laps

### SC Strategy Response

If SC deploys:
- Lap 1-15: Stay out if on mediums
- Lap 16-30: Pit if window open
- Lap 31+: Assess tire condition vs competition

## Weather Considerations

Race starts at dusk, finishing under lights:

- **Start time track temp**: ~32°C
- **End time track temp**: ~25°C

Temperature drop improves tire life in second stint.

## Historical Strategies

### 2023 Race

Winner strategy (Verstappen): Soft-Hard (1-stop)
- First stop: Lap 21
- Tire delta at finish: 1.2 sec advantage

### 2022 Race

Winner strategy (Verstappen): Medium-Hard (1-stop)
- First stop: Lap 18
- Extended final stint of 40 laps

## Strategic Recommendations

### For Leading Cars

1. Prioritize track position
2. Cover competitor pit stops
3. Use 1-stop if possible

### For Chasing Cars

1. Consider offset strategy
2. Undercut window: Laps 16-20
3. Fresh tire attack in final stint

### For Midfield

1. Aggressive 2-stop can work
2. Target clear air windows
3. Use pit stop to jump slower cars

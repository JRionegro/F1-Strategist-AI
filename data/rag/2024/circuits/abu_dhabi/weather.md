# Abu Dhabi Grand Prix - Weather Profile

## Climate Overview

Abu Dhabi in December offers consistent weather conditions:

- **Desert climate**: Very low rain probability
- **Twilight race**: Starts at sunset, finishes under lights
- **Temperature drop**: 5-8°C during race

## Historical Weather Data

### Race Day Conditions (5-year average)

| Metric | Start | Mid-Race | End |
|--------|-------|----------|-----|
| Air Temp | 27°C | 24°C | 22°C |
| Track Temp | 32°C | 27°C | 25°C |
| Humidity | 55% | 60% | 65% |
| Wind Speed | 8 km/h | 5 km/h | 3 km/h |

## Temperature Analysis

### Track Temperature Evolution

The race typically sees:

1. **Lap 1-15**: Peak track temp (30-35°C)
2. **Lap 16-35**: Transition period (27-30°C)
3. **Lap 36-58**: Cool conditions (24-27°C)

### Strategic Implications

Temperature drop affects:

- **Tire performance**: Better grip in cooler conditions
- **Engine cooling**: Less stress on power units
- **Fuel density**: Slightly improved efficiency

## Wind Patterns

### Prevailing Wind Direction

Primary wind: **NW (315°)** from the Persian Gulf

### Sector Impact

| Sector | Wind Effect |
|--------|-------------|
| Sector 1 | Headwind - reduces top speed |
| Sector 2 | Crosswind - affects stability |
| Sector 3 | Tailwind - assists acceleration |

## Rain Probability

Historical rain probability: **< 2%**

December is the driest month in Abu Dhabi:

- No recorded rain delays since 2009
- Track designed with minimal drainage
- Intermediate/wet compounds rarely used

## Humidity Effects

Higher humidity in evening affects:

- **Cooling efficiency**: Reduced radiator performance
- **Engine power**: Marginal reduction (~1-2 HP)
- **Tire rubber**: Slightly different grip levels

## Race-Specific Forecasts

### Typical Race Day

- **Formation lap**: 28°C air, 33°C track
- **Lap 20**: 25°C air, 29°C track
- **Final lap**: 22°C air, 25°C track

### Dew Point Considerations

Dew point typically around 15-18°C:

- Not reached during race
- No moisture condensation concerns
- Track remains consistently dry

## Weather Monitoring

### Key Indicators to Watch

1. **Wind direction shifts**: Can affect car balance
2. **Track temp at start**: Crucial for first stint
3. **Humidity trend**: Affects engine mapping

### Weather Stations

Circuit has weather stations at:
- Pit straight
- Sector 2 entry
- Back straight

Data updates every 30 seconds to teams.
